How to Play

Your goal is to click the blue target as many times as possible within 60 seconds while ignoring all distractions.

  • Click the blue circle with the bullseye to earn points
  • Avoid clicking the colorful distractions that appear around the screen
  • Each correct click gives you +10 points
  • Each distraction clicked gives you -5 points
  • The game gets harder each level with more distractions and faster movement
  • Complete all 5 levels to win the game!
